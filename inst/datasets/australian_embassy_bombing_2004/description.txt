These data were made available through the John Jay & ARTIS Transnational Terrorism Database (JJATT), which hosts a collection network datasets pertaining to radical Islamists and their associates who have directly contributed to a terrorist attack. The present data depict a time series of a network of individuals involved in the 2004 Australian Embassy Bombing in Jakarta, Indonesia. The network is composed of individuals and the evolving relationships among these. As such, this series is useful for exploring compositional network changes in a terrorist network over time.
