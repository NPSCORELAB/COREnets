These data depict the network of individuals involved in the 2000 bombing of the Philippine consulate in Menteng, Jakarta, Indonesia. This incident was perpetrated by Jemaah Islamiyah, a terrorist organization linked to a string of terrorist attacks in Southeast Asia, and claimed the lives of 2 people while injuring 21 others. The authors of these dataset, JJATT, gathered these data from using 'open source' information. The present dataset reflects a combination of familial, frienship, and association relationships. Furthermore, as a dynamic dataset these relationships are depicted as dynamic and subject to changesover time.
