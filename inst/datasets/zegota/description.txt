This 268 node network represents a multitude of Polish resistance organization in Nazi occupied Poland (1942 -1945).  Recreated for the Master's Thesis "UW Conceptulization of Resistance Networks: Illuminating 21st Century Uncertainty" by Nguanyade S. Kemokay, and Thomas Ludwig, this network describes the individual's membership to resistance organizations, membership to Zegota Operational Network, the individual's region, skill possessed, role within their respective organizations, individual's post-uprising status, and political parties, and region.  A codebook is provided with a scale for each attribute and description of each attribute's meaning.
