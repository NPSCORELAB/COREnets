This network depicts a the ties between individuals associated with Jemaah Islamiyah and involed in one of the following events: Christmas Eve bombings in 2000, Philippines Ambassador Residence bombing in 2000, Bali bombings in 2002, Australian Embassy bombing in 2005, and Bali bombing in 2005.

The authors of these dataset, JJATT, gathered these data from using "open source" information. The present dataset reflects a combination of familial, frienship, and association relationships. Furthermore, as a dynamic dataset these relationships are depicted as dynamic and subject to changes.
