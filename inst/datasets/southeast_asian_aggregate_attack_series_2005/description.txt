These data were made available through the John Jay & ARTIS Transnational Terrorism Database (JJATT), which hosts a collection network datasets pertaining to radical Islamists and their associates who have directly contributed to a terrorist attack. The present data set represents the relationships between actors associated with Jemaah Islamiyah and involved in one or mulitiple terrorist incidents (e.g., Christmas Eve bombings in 2000, Philippines Ambassador Residence bombing in 2000, Bali bombings in 2002, Australian Embassy bombing in 2005, and Bali bombing in 2005).
